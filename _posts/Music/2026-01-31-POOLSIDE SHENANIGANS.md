POOLSIDE SHENANIGANS is the first (and only as of yet) album from [SUPER VIOLENT EXPLODING SUN](../SUPER-VIOLENT-EXPLODING-SUN). The tracks were all written by Matteo, the band's singer, and arranged by the whole band. The 13 tracks were written and prepared in a little over one year of the band's existence.
