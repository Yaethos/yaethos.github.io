Camera Martinez EP is a small musical project developed with my roommates in 2026. It comes from a few ironic songs that were written by them during the last few years. After the purchase of a SM57 microphone, I decided to give it a test run by producing and recording the songs with a very light and for-fun approach.

The EP is still in progress, and features 5 songs in total where I've handled production, recording, mix and master. The EP spans across different genres, from surf punk to gospel rap and will release on soundcloud.
